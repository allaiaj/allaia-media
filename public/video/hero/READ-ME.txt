HERO MARQUEE CLIPS
==================
Every MP4 dropped in this folder scrolls across the top of the
brand-collabs page as a moving marquee - autoplay, muted, looping.

Just drop the files in (any name). The marquee sorts them alphabetically
and hides itself entirely until at least one clip is here, so nothing
looks broken while it is empty.

Best results: short vertical clips (6-10s, 9:16), full quality - I
compress them here to ~200-400KB each. Six or more fills the strip
without an obvious repeat.
